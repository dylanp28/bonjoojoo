## EMAIL & COMMUNICATION LUXURY LANGUAGE

### Welcome Email Subject Lines
- "Welcome to the bonjoojoo Atelier"
- "Your Journey in Conscious Luxury Begins"
- "Discover Where Light Becomes Legend"

### Email Copy Style Guide
```
Salutation: "Dear [Name]," (never "Hey" or "Hi")

Opening: Always acknowledge their choice to join us
Example: "Thank you for choosing to explore conscious luxury with bonjoojoo."

Body: Sophisticated but warm, educational without being preachy

Closing: "With appreciation," or "In light and brilliance,"

Signature: 
The bonjoojoo Atelier Team
Where Light Becomes Legend
```
