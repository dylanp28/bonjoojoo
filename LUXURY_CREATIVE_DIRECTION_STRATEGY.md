## 2. SOPHISTICATED VISUAL NARRATIVE

### PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

#### Hero Photography Standards
1. **Lighting Setup**:
   - Museum-quality lighting with controlled shadows
   - Temperature: 5500K-6500K for diamond brilliance
   - Multiple lighting angles to show fire and scintillation
   - Background gradients from pure white to subtle cream

2. **Composition Rules**:
   - Rule of thirds for dynamic placement
   - Negative space for luxury breathing room
   - Macro details revealing craftsmanship
   - Environmental context showing scale and elegance

3. **Model Direction**:
   - Hands: Elegant positioning, professional manicures
   - Expression: Subtle confidence, not over-enthusiastic
   - Styling: Minimalist, letting jewelry be the hero
   - Diversity: Global luxury appeal across ethnicities

#### Visual Storytelling Concepts

**Concept 1: "Alchemy of Light"**
- Document diamond creation process artistically
- Laboratory as modern atelier
- Science as art form
- Clean, sterile beauty of creation

**Concept 2: "Modern Heirloom"**
- Jewelry in architectural settings
- Clean lines, luxury materials (marble, brass, velvet)
- Generational storytelling through styling
- Timeless pieces in contemporary contexts

**Concept 3: "Conscious Radiance"**
- Nature-inspired but not literal
- Sustainable luxury messaging
- Clean, bright, optimistic aesthetic
- Innovation as natural evolution

### LUXURY COLOR PSYCHOLOGY

#### Primary Palette: "Cultivated Elegance"
- **Diamond White**: #FAFAFA (pure, honest, brilliant)
- **Platinum**: #E5E4E2 (sophisticated, valuable, enduring)
- **Champagne Gold**: #F7E7CE (warm luxury, accessible elegance)
- **Deep Charcoal**: #36454F (serious luxury, professional)

#### Secondary Palette: "Emotional Resonance"
- **Blush Rose**: #F5E6E8 (romantic, feminine, soft luxury)
- **Sage Green**: #B2BEB5 (sustainable, natural, calming)
- **Warm Taupe**: #8B8680 (earthy luxury, grounded)
- **Ivory**: #FFFFF0 (classic, timeless, pure)

#### Usage Guidelines:
- **70%**: Whites and neutrals for luxury breathing room
- **20%**: Charcoal for typography and accents
- **10%**: Color accents for emotional moments

### ICONOGRAPHY & GRAPHIC ELEMENTS

#### Custom Icon System
1. **Diamond Icons**: Geometric interpretations of diamond cuts
2. **Craftsmanship**: Tools rendered as luxury objects
3. **Sustainability**: Growth patterns, molecular structures
4. **Service**: Concierge-level service visualization

#### Graphic Language
- **Lines**: Clean, architectural, precise
- **Shapes**: Geometric with soft corners
- **Patterns**: Diamond-inspired but abstract
- **Texture**: Subtle, refined, never busy
