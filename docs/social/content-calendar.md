## Part 1 — Brand Voice & Content Pillars

### The 5 bonjoojoo Content Pillars

| # | Pillar | Core Message | Tone |
|---|--------|-------------|------|
| 1 | **Minimal Luxury** | Understated elegance. Aspirational, never pretentious. | Refined, clean, editorial |
| 2 | **Ethical Choice** | Lab-grown = zero mining, same diamond, no compromise. | Confident, fact-driven, purposeful |
| 3 | **The Milestone Moment** | Engagements, anniversaries, push presents, self-love rituals. | Warm, emotional, celebratory |
| 4 | **Self-Expression** | Your jewelry, your story. Stack it. Wear it your way. | Playful, personal, confident |
| 5 | **Diamond Intelligence** | GIA-graded. 4Cs educated. You deserve to know what you're buying. | Trustworthy, transparent, empowering |

### Brand Voice Rules
- **Do:** Write like a brilliant, values-driven friend who happens to know everything about diamonds.
- **Do:** Lead with emotion or a curiosity hook, earn the product mention.
- **Do:** Educate without lecturing. Make people feel smart, not talked down to.
- **Don't:** Use hypey, salesy language ("Get it now before it's gone!").
- **Don't:** Overuse exclamation points — one per caption max.
- **Don't:** Say "mined diamonds are bad" — say "lab-grown means better for everyone."

### Caption Formula
```
[Hook — first line stops the scroll]
[Story/value paragraph]
[Product context]
[CTA — clear single action]
```
