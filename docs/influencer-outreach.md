## Campaign Notes

- **Gift budget:** $200/influencer × 10 = $2,000 max outreach investment
- **Expected conversion:** 30–50% reply rate for cold DMs at this tier; 20–30% convert to post
- **Timeline:** Send DMs Day 1 → Follow-up Day 6 → Evaluate responses Day 10
- **Gifted pieces:** Prioritize hero SKUs — engagement-adjacent or stackable rings, statement earrings
- **Tracking:** When a post goes live, add coupon code `[HANDLE]10` for 10% off to measure referral traffic
- **Next step:** If 3+ posts go live, escalate to paid partnership tier for top performers
