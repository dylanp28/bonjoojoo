## Implementation Notes for Klaviyo Setup

### Segmentation Tags
- `welcome_series`: Triggered by new subscriber event
- `abandoned_cart`: Trigger: "Started Checkout" → no "Order Placed" within 60 min
- `post_purchase`: Trigger: "Order Placed" Klaviyo metric
- `winback`: Segment: "Has placed order" AND "Last Active > 90 days"

### A/B Test Recommendations
| Email | Test Variable | Hypothesis |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome E1 | Subject with vs. without emoji | Emoji increases open rate |
| Abandoned E1 | 1hr vs. 30min send time | Faster = higher recovery rate |
| Abandoned E3 | 10% off vs. free gift with purchase | Gift may outperform discount |
| Win-back E1 | "We miss you" vs. product-led subject | Product-led may drive more clicks |

### Suppression Rules
- Unsubscribers suppressed across all flows
- Purchasers exit abandoned cart flow immediately
- Active customers (purchased in 30 days) excluded from win-back
