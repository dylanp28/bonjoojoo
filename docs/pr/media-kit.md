### bonjoojoo Launches Lab-Grown Diamond Luxury Jewelry — Fine Jewelry Reimagined for the Conscious Generation

*New direct-to-consumer brand offers LVMH-level craftsmanship with ethically cultivated diamonds at 60–80% below traditional fine jewelry prices*

**[City, Month 2026]** — bonjoojoo, a new luxury fine jewelry brand built for the conscious consumer, today announced its public launch at bonjoojoo.com. The brand offers curated collections of lab-grown diamond rings, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings — all set in 14k gold — at a price point that democratizes fine jewelry without compromising quality, ethics, or beauty.

"We built bonjoojoo for the generation that refuses to choose between stunning and responsible," said the bonjoojoo founder. "Every piece we create starts with the same question: what does it mean to make something truly beautiful today? The answer is always the same — it means making it without compromise."

bonjoojoo launches with five signature collections spanning engagement rings, tennis bracelets, diamond pendants, drop earrings, and stacking bands. Entry prices begin at $485 for the Classic Gold Band Bracelet and extend to over $2,400 for the Diamond Eternity Tennis Bracelet — a fraction of the cost of equivalent mined-diamond pieces. All diamonds are IGI- and GIA-certified, and every design is available in yellow, white, and rose 14k gold.

Unlike legacy fine jewelry retailers, bonjoojoo operates exclusively direct-to-consumer, passing the savings of a leaner supply chain directly to customers while offering concierge-level service and a 30-day return policy with lifetime warranty on all pieces.

The lab-grown diamond market is growing at 14.15% CAGR and is forecast to exceed $37 billion by 2030. Millennials and Gen Z now represent the fastest-growing segment of fine jewelry buyers — and they are driving demand for brands that align with their values. bonjoojoo is positioned squarely at the intersection of this shift: luxury design, ethical sourcing, and transparent pricing.

"Lab-grown diamonds are chemically, physically, and optically identical to mined diamonds," the founder added. "The only difference is the story — ours is one we're proud to tell."

bonjoojoo's collections are available now at **bonjoojoo.com**. The brand ships nationwide and offers complimentary gift packaging on all orders.
