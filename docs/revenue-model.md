## 4. Ad Spend ROI — $80/Day Launch Budget

**Total monthly ad spend:** $80/day × 30 days = **$2,400/month**

### Gross Margin Assumptions

| Cost Item | Estimate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| COGS (product + packaging) | 35% of revenue | Lab-grown diamonds, 14k gold |
| Payment processing (Stripe) | 2.9% + $0.30/order | ~$35/order at $1,200 AOV |
| Shipping | $15–25/order | Free shipping threshold incentivizes larger orders |
| **Gross margin (post-COGS + fulfillment)** | **~55–60%** | Before marketing costs |
| **Contribution margin** | **~$660–720/order** | At $1,200 AOV |

### ROAS Scenarios at $2,400/Month Spend

| ROAS | Revenue Generated | Orders (at $1,200 AOV) | Net from Ads |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Breakeven (2.02x)** | $4,848 | 4.0 orders | $0 net (covers COGS + fulfillment) |
| **2x ROAS** | $4,800 | 4.0 orders | Marginal loss ($48) |
| **3x ROAS** | $7,200 | 6.0 orders | **+$2,640 gross profit** |
| **4x ROAS** | $9,600 | 8.0 orders | **+$5,280 gross profit** |

**Breakeven calculation:**
- Contribution margin = ~57% of revenue = $684/order
- Orders needed to cover $2,400 ad spend: $2,400 ÷ $684 = **3.5 orders**
- Revenue at breakeven: 3.5 × $1,200 = **$4,200**
- Breakeven ROAS: $4,200 ÷ $2,400 = **1.75x**

> **Target:** 2.5x ROAS minimum in Month 1. At 2.5x, ad spend generates $6,000 revenue = $3,420 gross profit. $1,020 goes to covering ad spend and you pocket $1,020 net.

### Orders/Month from Paid Only at Different ROAS

| ROAS | Revenue from Paid | Orders from Paid | Contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1.75x (breakeven) | $4,200 | 3.5 | Break even on ad spend |
| 2x | $4,800 | 4.0 | +$336 above ad spend |
| 2.5x | $6,000 | 5.0 | **+$1,020 above ad spend** |

## 6. Summary: Month 1 Minimum Viable Numbers

To be **on track for $5K MRR by Month 3**, Month 1 must establish these baselines:

### Month 1 Success Criteria

| Metric | Month 1 Target | Month 2 Target | Month 3 Target ($5K MRR) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Revenue** | $1,200–2,400 (1–2 orders) | $2,400–4,800 (2–4 orders) | **$5,000+ (5+ orders)** |
| **Sessions/month** | 300–500 | 400–600 | 500–750 |
| **CVR** | 0.5–1% (baseline) | 1–1.5% (optimized) | 1–2% (warmed up) |
| **AOV** | $1,200 (base) | $1,200–1,500 | $1,200–1,500 |
| **Email list** | 50–100 subscribers | 200–400 subscribers | 400–600 subscribers |
| **Paid ROAS** | ≥1.75x (breakeven) | ≥2.5x | ≥3x |
| **Blended CAC** | $500–700 | $400–500 | $300–400 |

### Month 1 Non-Negotiables

1. **Stripe live** — No revenue without payments. This is the critical path blocker.
2. **Meta pixel installed** — Every visitor must be pixeled for retargeting.
3. **Google Analytics 4 + conversion tracking** — Cannot optimize without data.
4. **Email capture active** — Popup or inline signup, target 15%+ opt-in rate.
5. **At least 3 Meta ad creatives live** — Test product + lifestyle + testimonial formats.
6. **Google Shopping feed submitted** — High-intent, low-cost traffic channel.

### 3-Month Ramp Plan

```
Month 1 — Foundation
├── Revenue target: $1,200 (1 order minimum to validate funnel)
├── Ad budget: $80/day = $2,400
├── Focus: Test creative, install tracking, build email list
├── Win condition: 1 paid order + CVR data baseline
└── If no orders by Day 21: audit site UX + checkout flow

Month 2 — Optimize
├── Revenue target: $3,600 (3 orders)
├── Ad budget: $80/day (hold, optimize allocation)
├── Focus: Scale winning ad sets, retarget pixel audience, 1st email sequence
├── Win condition: ROAS > 2x, 3 paid orders, 200 email subscribers
└── Launch influencer micro-partnership (1-2 creators, 10K-50K followers)

Month 3 — Scale
├── Revenue target: $5,000+ (5 orders)
├── Ad budget: $100–120/day if ROAS > 2.5x in Month 2
├── Focus: Expand to Google Shopping, referral program, email flows
├── Win condition: 5+ orders, ROAS > 3x, 500 email subscribers
└── KPI: LTV:CAC > 3x across all channels
```
