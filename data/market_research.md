# Jewelry Market Research & Competitive Analysis

## Executive Summary

The lab-grown diamond market is experiencing explosive growth, with lab-grown diamonds now costing 73% less than natural diamonds as of 2025. Average engagement ring spending has declined from $6,000 in 2021 to $5,200 in 2024, driven primarily by the accessibility of lab-grown alternatives.

## Lab-Grown Diamond Market Trends 2024-2025

### Pricing Insights
- **Cost Advantage**: Lab-grown diamonds cost 60-80% less than natural diamonds
- **1-carat lab-grown engagement rings**: $1,500-$3,000 (vs. $5,000-$15,000 natural)
- **Average engagement ring price**: $5,200 (down from $6,000 in 2021)
- **Predicted trend**: Most lab diamonds will sell for under $1,000 by 2026
- **Premium positioning**: BonJooJoo targets $300+ average order value

### Popular Styles & Trends

#### 2025 Engagement Ring Trends
1. **Bezel Settings**: Increased demand for modern, secure settings
2. **Mixed Metals**: Two-tone gold combinations (white/rose, yellow/white)
3. **Colored Diamonds**: Salt-and-pepper, champagne, and fancy yellow diamonds
4. **Double Halos**: Maximum sparkle with layered diamond halos
5. **Vintage Revival**: Art Deco-inspired designs with milgrain details
6. **Asymmetric Settings**: Breaking traditional symmetry rules
7. **Colored Gemstone Accents**: Sapphires and emeralds paired with diamonds
8. **Bold Settings**: Substantial, architectural designs
9. **Portrait & Rose Cut Diamonds**: Alternative cuts for unique appeal
10. **Micro-Pavé Everything**: Full band coverage with tiny diamonds

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors

#### 1. Brilliant Earth
- **Positioning**: Ethical luxury, established market leader
- **Price range**: $800-$15,000+
- **Strengths**: Strong brand recognition, extensive inventory
- **Weaknesses**: Higher pricing, less personal service
- **Market share**: ~15% of online lab-grown market

#### 2. Clean Origin
- **Positioning**: Pure lab-grown focus, competitive pricing
- **Price range**: $600-$8,000
- **Strengths**: Transparent pricing, good value
- **Weaknesses**: Limited brand story, basic designs
- **Market share**: ~8% of online lab-grown market

#### 3. James Allen
- **Positioning**: Technology-focused, 360° diamond viewing
- **Price range**: $500-$12,000
- **Strengths**: Virtual try-on, extensive customization
- **Weaknesses**: Overwhelming choice, impersonal experience
- **Market share**: ~12% of online engagement market

#### 4. Blue Nile
- **Positioning**: Value leader, both natural and lab-grown
- **Price range**: $400-$20,000+
- **Strengths**: Massive selection, competitive pricing
- **Weaknesses**: Commodity approach, weak brand story
- **Market share**: ~20% of online diamond market
